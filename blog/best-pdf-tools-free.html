Best Online PDF Tools for Free

PDF remains the universal language of documents. Whether it's a contract, an eBook, or a university assignment, the PDF format ensures your formatting stays exactly as intended. However, editing and managing these files can often be difficult without the right tools. Most "pro" PDF software is expensive, but the web offers powerful free alternatives.

Merging and Splitting with Ease

Sometimes you have five different documents that need to be one. Other times, you have a massive manual and only need one page. Tools like our Merge PDF and Split PDF utilities allow you to reorganize your documents in seconds.

The Power of Compression

Large PDF files can be a pain to email. Using a PDF Compressor can reduce your file size by half or more without significantly affecting the readability of text or the quality of images.
