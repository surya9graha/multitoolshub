Under the Hood: Privacy & Performance

Privacy is the central design tenet of MultiTools Hub. PDF compilation, file splitting, and rotation are performed by high-performance JavaScript engines (like pdf-lib or WebAssembly builds of document libraries) operating entirely in your client RAM. Files never touch the cloud. By eliminating server uploads, we block data interception, reduce page load times, and offer a tool that works regardless of bandwidth limits once cached. Standard security protocols are implemented to keep operations sandboxed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a limit on the file size of PDF uploads?

A: Our tools run locally in browser memory, meaning size limits depend on your computer's RAM. We recommend files under 50MB for smooth, lag-free processing on standard machines.

Q: Can I merge password-protected PDF files?

A: You must unlock secured PDF documents first using our Unlock PDF tool before they can be merged or split with other files.

Q: Are my sensitive contracts or financial PDFs secure here?

A: Absolutely. We do not transfer your documents over the internet. The processing starts, runs, and finishes on your computer. This makes it safe for sensitive corporate and personal documents.
