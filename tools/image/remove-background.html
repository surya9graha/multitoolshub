How to Use the AI Background Remover

  1. Upload: Click the upload zone to select your image or drag and drop your file (.jpg, .png, or .webp).
  2. AI Analysis: Our local AI model (MediaPipe) will automatically detect the subject and generate a masking layer.
  3. Fine-Tune (Optional): Use the Manual Erase Brush at the top to clean up any complex edges the AI might have missed.
  4. Compare: Use the Compare toggle to see your result against the original for precise quality control.
  5. Download: Once satisfied, click the download button to save your transparent PNG asset.
