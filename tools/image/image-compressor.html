Compression FAQ

What is the difference between lossy and lossless compression?

Lossy compression (like JPG) removes some data to achieve smaller sizes, while lossless compression (like PNG-8) maintains all data. Our tool uses intelligent lossy algorithms to provide the best balance for web usage.

Will my compressed images look blurry?

If you keep the quality above 70-80%, the difference is usually invisible to the naked eye on standard monitors. We recommend testing different levels for your specific needs.
