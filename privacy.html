Privacy Policy

At MultiTools Hub, we take your privacy seriously. We want you to understand how we handle your data and what measures we take to protect your information when using our 100+ online tools.

Information Collection

Actually, we collect very little information from you. For the vast majority of our tools, all processing is done locally in your browser. This means that if you use our Image Resizer or JSON Formatter, your data—whether it's an image or code—is not uploaded to our servers. We believe in providing a platform that is safe and private by design.

Analytics and Cookies

We do use standard analytic tools like Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. This helps us decide which tools to improve and which new categories to add. These tools may use cookies to gather anonymous data like your browser type, device type, and the pages you visit.

Advertisements

To keep our tools free, we display advertisements. Our advertising partners, such as Google AdSense, may use cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your Google Ad Settings.

Contacting Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us through our Contact Us page. Your feedback is always welcome and helps us build a better experience for everyone.
