## JPG to PNG
Convert your JPG images to PNG format instantly while preserving transparency and quality. While JPG is great for photographs due to its small file size, it doesn't support transparency and uses lossy compression that can lead to artifacts. PNG, on the other hand, is a lossless format that supports alpha channels, making it the industry standard for logos, icons, and graphics with transparent backgrounds. Our converter ensures that the transition between these formats is seamless. Simply upload your JPG, and our tool will re-encode it as a high-quality PNG. This is particularly useful for designers who need to add transparency to a graphic or for developers who require lossless assets for their projects.
